Today's Recipe: PANEER KOHLAPURI

A typical Maharashtrian taste profile, with very rich, complex spice notes, and flavourful aroma.Paneer Kolhapuri is a spicy and delicious curry made with Indian cottage cheese, lot of spices, coconut, onions and tomatoes. The recipe is from the spicy Kolhapuri cuisine served with some amti dal and kachumber.

Total Time: 20-30 mins

Ingrediants:

  • Coconut: 1 cup(70 gms approx), grated
  • Dry Red Chilies: 4 pieces
  • Onion: 2, finely chopped
  • Paneer: 750 gms, cut into cubes
  • Caeser Dressing

Preparation:

  1. Firstly, in a pan dry roast all the spices listed for masala on low flame for 2 minutes.
  2. Blend to fine powder and kolhapuri masala is ready. keep aside.
  3. Heat 3 tbsp oil and fry 9 cubes paneer. keep aside.

    4. Time: 5 mins

  4. In the same oil add in 1 bay leaf, onion, ginger garlic paste and saute well.

    5. Time: 3-4 mins

  5. Add 3 tomato and cook well.

    6. Time: 10 mins

  6. Mash the tomatoes well with the help of potato masher.
  7. Add in prepared kolhapuri masala, Â¼ tsp turmeric and 1 tsp salt.
  8. Saute on low flame till oil separates from masala.

    9. Time: 3-4 mins

  9. Now add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting consistency.
  10. Add in fried paneer.

    11. Time: 2-3 mins

  11. Cover and simmer.

    12. Time: 5 mins

  12. Now add 1 tsp crushed kasuri methi and mix well.
  13. Finally serve paneer kolhapuri masala with tandoori roti or garlic naan.
